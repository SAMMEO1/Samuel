About me

My name is Samuel Gustafsson Sandkvist. I was born in 1995 in Stockholm Sweden. I have been studying graphic- web design and web development at Sjödalsgymnasiet and Tumba gymnasium since 2014. I’m a freelance graphic designer branding myself as “Sam Design”, I take orders through social media and e-mail or you can check out my store with pre-made products. My main areas of work so far have been social media banners and commercial work for Internet profiles such as “RonsGamertag” with over 30,000 YouTube subscribers and “Destiny Dynasty” closing up on 10,000.
